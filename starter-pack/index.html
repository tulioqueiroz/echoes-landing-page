About

What is Echoes?

Echoes is where I build a body of work that explores the things that feel too big to keep inside — the moments that change us, the people we can't forget, the versions of ourselves we had to leave behind to become who we are now.

I write flash fiction — ultra-short stories, usually 100–300 words — because sometimes the smallest moments hold the deepest truths.

For those who feel deeply and aren't afraid of the dark parts.
